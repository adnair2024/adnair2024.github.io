    the beginning.

    December 20, 2025

    This is my first post. I’ve been trying to make a public blog on GH pages for such a long time, and I never got around to doing it. Now, I can finally say “ha check my blog”, and mean it.

    I’m currently using Hugo as the brain for my blog, and a theme called hugo-brewm. It looks really nice, and I had Gemini replicate the demo layout because I found it really pretty. Took a couple hours of work, and I spent that time drinking coffee and coding in a small coffee shop downstairs from the apartment.

    Above is a picture of the aforementioned coffee.

    This blog is meant to be a beautiful and brutalist peak into my brain, inspired by the design (and home decor) philosophy of Rick Owens. I’ve been reading a lot about how Owens thinks and performs daily activities, and I think its a nice and clean way to go about life. I’m going to have to wait to make setup content until I’m back home, but for now, I’ll go ahead and brainstorm some other ideas for blog posts. Maybe about Zelda: Tears of The Kingdom?
